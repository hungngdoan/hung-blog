All-Time Favorites

Mega Man Zero (1-4) Capcom / Inti Creates

Peak action platformer. Zero's story across all four games is one of gaming's best arcs — from a broken weapon waking up in a cave to the final sacrifice in Zero 4. The gameplay is tight, the music is incredible, and the pixel art is timeless. This series made me fall in love with games.

★★★★★
GBA #action #platformer
Teamobi Games Teamobi

Childhood nostalgia. Ninja School, Avatar, and the whole Teamobi universe shaped my love for games growing up in Vietnam. Those tiny Java phone screens running these massive RPG worlds — pure magic. The community forums were my first internet home.

★★★★★
Mobile #RPG #nostalgia

Beautiful, melancholic, and brutally hard. Hallownest is one of the most atmospheric game worlds ever built. The Pantheon of Hallownest broke me — 42 bosses in a row with no checkpoints. Still haven't beaten it. Still thinking about it.

The combat system is perfection. No hiding behind a shield, no grinding levels — pure skill. Isshin Ashina is the best final boss in gaming. The moment the deflect rhythm clicks is the most satisfying thing in any game I've played.
